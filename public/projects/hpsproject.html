Project Description

Created a website that would help After School Programs and Public Schools better communicate their student's data. This website includes a user log in authentication for teachers and after school staffs to log in. A activity log's page to log the staff's activities such as submitting disciplinary/incident forms in real-time. Student's behavior page to keep track of their progress by converting their grades into graph representations. A submission forms page for staffs to upload any disciplinary or incident that happened throughout the day.

Technologies

  • Flask
  • MySQL
  • HTML5,CSS3
